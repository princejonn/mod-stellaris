[h1]City World Start[/h1]

When you feel the need to purge in the name of the emperor, you need to start from a position of strength. This start will have you on Holy Terra, a city world, with two extra colonies on Mars and Venus. It is definitely easy mode, but when the xeno filth must be destroyed, does it really matter?

[h1]Want to start your leader as the chosen one?[/h1]
I suggest you also download [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1271263414]Custom Leaders[/url] so that you can set that up. It's how I've played my 40K inspired Imperium of Man games.
