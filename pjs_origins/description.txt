Adding new origins to the game.

[h1]New Origins[/h1]
[list]
    [*] [b]Adrift[/b] - Start on a barren wasteland that has been drifting in space, and is now caught in the grip of a black hole.
    [*] [b]Cradle of Creation[/b] - Start on a gaia world with access to many more gaia worlds nearby.
    [*] [b]Envy[/b] - Start on a normal world that's circling a ring world.
    [*] [b]Parasitic Origins[/b] - Has the ability to assimilate new species into the hive mind at the start of the game.
    [*] [b]Holy Terra[/b] - Start on the sacred city planet of Holy Terra (Earth). Begin with two extra colonies on Venus and Mars.
    [*] [b]Loved by the Void[/b] - Start in a trinary star system with a multitude of (eventually) habitable planets.
    [*] [b]Precursor[/b] - Start on a city planet with access to immense resources in the nearby systems. Ruined megastructures are in the vicinity. This will give you a very easy start.
    [*] [b]Solio Aeternitatis[/b] - Start on a ringworld orbiting a wormhole with other ringworlds and advantageous systems nearby. Ruined megastructures are in the vicinity. This will give you a significant start advantage.
    [*] [b]Warden[/b] - Start on a ring world with access to immense resources in the nearby systems. Ruined megastructures are in the vicinity. This will give you a very easy start.
[/list]
