This mod allows you to customize all of your leaders. You will have a special project in your situation log (which will reappear) where you are able to initiate the change.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
   [*] Start with the chosen one ruler from the start and roleplay to your hearts content!
   [*] Clear up any negative traits that your leaders have somehow decided to pick up.
   [*] Add new traits to all of your leaders.
   [*] Add varying amounts of experience points to your leaders.
   [*] Add and customize as much as you want. Press done when you're done.
[/list]

[h1]Compability[/h1]
No issues.
